Sales Data Analysis

In this project, I analyzed and visualized sales data using Python to identify key sales trends, customer behavior, and business performance. I explored seasonal sales patterns, product performance, and geographic trends through various data visualizations such as bar charts and time series plots. This project highlights my data cleaning, analysis, and visualization skills.

This Sales Data Analysis project provided actionable insights that helped the business make data-driven decisions. By identifying key sales trends, such as peak sales periods and top-performing products, the business was able to optimize inventory management and marketing strategies. Geographic analysis of sales patterns enabled the company to target specific regions more effectively, while customer behavior insights allowed for personalized promotions and improved customer retention. The visualizations from this project, including time series plots and bar charts, helped stakeholders quickly grasp performance metrics, leading to increased profitability and overall business efficiency.

The analysis provided insights into popular pizzas, peak ordering times, and customer preferences. This project helped me develop strong SQL skills, including querying, aggregation, and deriving actionable insights from transactional data.

This pizza sales analysis enhanced my ability to work with large datasets and extract meaningful insights, which directly contributed to my understanding of consumer behavior and operational performance. It allowed me to improve my skills in data aggregation, filtering, and joining complex tables to make data-driven decisions, such as identifying the best-selling pizzas and optimal staffing during peak hours. Additionally, it strengthened my proficiency in SQL, making me more adept at solving real-world business problems through structured data analysis.

HR Analytics Dashboard

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive view of employee attrition, satisfaction, and performance across various departments. I created visualizations to highlight trends in workforce management, including job satisfaction, overtime, and employee retention, enabling data-driven HR decision-making.

This HR Analytics Dashboard provided actionable insights that helped the organization make informed decisions regarding employee management. By visualizing key metrics such as employee attrition, job satisfaction, and overtime, HR teams were able to identify potential areas of concern, such as departments with higher turnover or low job satisfaction. The dashboard also highlighted the impact of overtime on employee retention, enabling management to implement strategies to improve work-life balance. These insights ultimately helped optimize workforce planning, enhance employee engagement, and reduce overall attrition rates.

Hotel Management Dashboard

I built a Power BI dashboard to analyze hotel booking and customer data. The dashboard includes insights such as room occupancy rates, revenue per room, customer segmentation, and seasonal booking trends. It provides hotel management with an overview of performance metrics and helps in making operational decisions.

This hotel booking dashboard provided critical insights that helped management optimize operations and increase profitability. By analyzing room occupancy rates and revenue per room, the dashboard allowed for better forecasting and pricing strategies. Customer segmentation data helped tailor marketing campaigns to different demographics, while insights into seasonal booking trends enabled the hotel to adjust staffing and inventory accordingly. These data-driven decisions improved overall efficiency, maximized room utilization, and enhanced the guest experience, ultimately leading to increased revenue and customer satisfaction.

Olympic Data Analysis

This project involved analyzing data from the Olympic Games to explore trends in athlete performance, medal distribution, and country rankings. Using Python, I performed data cleaning, exploratory data analysis, and visualized patterns in various sports events and athlete demographics.

This project provided valuable insights into the Olympic Games by revealing trends in athlete performance and medal distribution across different countries. It helped stakeholders understand which sports and countries consistently perform well, enabling better resource allocation and training strategies. The analysis also uncovered patterns in athlete demographics, such as age and gender distribution, allowing organizations to focus on specific groups for talent development. These findings contributed to a more data-driven approach to decision-making in sports management and athlete preparation.
